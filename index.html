PORTFOLIO

Since I was very young, I've loved working in, around, and with computers. Through middle school, I had a knack for persuading my teachers to spend my study time in the computer lab. In high school I took advantage of the opportunity to take the computer classes. In college, I majored in Computer Science and Information Technology. I was disappointed, however, to realize that my degrees didn't teach me what I wanted to know. It wasn't until I began working In the School of Information Sciences and Learning Technologies (SISLT) that I found the opportunity I was looking for.

SISLT opened doors that I had never fathomed.
